# INTENT COMMERCE — Competition Video Script
## "The Future of E-Commerce is Conversational"

**Target Duration:** 2 minutes 30 seconds  
**Format:** Voiceover + Animated Visual Slides  
**Tone:** Innovative, visionary, confident

---

## SCENE 1: THE HOOK (0:00 — 0:15)
**Visual:** Fast montage of frustrating e-commerce experiences — endless scrolling, confusing filters, abandoned carts, 20 open tabs.
**Text on screen:** "82% of online shoppers abandon their carts."

**VOICEOVER:**
"Every day, millions of shoppers struggle to find what they need. Endless scrolling. Confusing filters. Twenty browser tabs. And still — nothing. E-commerce today is broken."

---

## SCENE 2: THE PROBLEM (0:15 — 0:30)
**Visual:** Split screen. Left: User frustrated at computer. Right: Vendor drowning in spreadsheets.
**Text on screen:** "Shoppers can't find products. Vendors can't predict demand."

**VOICEOVER:**
"On one side, shoppers waste hours searching. On the other, vendors guess what to stock, when to restock, and why sales suddenly drop. The gap between intention and action has never been wider."

---

## SCENE 3: THE SOLUTION — INTENT COMMERCE (0:30 — 0:45)
**Visual:** Clean, futuristic UI appears. A chat bubble opens. Sleek product cards animate in.
**Text on screen:** "INTENT COMMERCE" (logo reveal with particle effect)

**VOICEOVER:**
"Introducing Intent Commerce. The world's first fully conversational, AI-powered multivendor marketplace. You don't browse. You simply... talk."

---

## SCENE 4: CONVERSATIONAL SHOPPING (0:45 — 1:10)
**Visual:** Screen recording style — chat interface.
- User types: "I need comfortable running shoes for marathons under $100"
- AI instantly replies with 3 product cards, images, prices
- User says: "Add the red ones to my cart"
- Cart updates instantly
- User says: "Checkout"
- Order summary appears, user confirms

**Text overlays:** "Just talk. We'll handle the rest."

**VOICEOVER:**
"Our intelligent shopping agent understands natural language, remembers your preferences, and handles everything from discovery to checkout. Ask for red running shoes under a hundred dollars — and watch the perfect options appear instantly. Add to cart, confirm payment, done."

---

## SCENE 5: THE AI BRAIN — MULTIMODAL RAG (1:10 — 1:25)
**Visual:** Abstract visualization of the RAG pipeline.
- User query splits into text and image paths
- Text flows through BGE-M3 embedding
- Image flows through SigLIP embedding  
- Vectors converge in Qdrant
- Hybrid fusion animation
- Top products surface

**Text on screen:** "Hybrid Multimodal RAG | Dense + Sparse + Visual Fusion"

**VOICEOVER:**
"Under the hood, our hybrid multimodal RAG pipeline combines text understanding with visual recognition. BGE-M3 for semantic meaning. SigLIP for image similarity. Fused through reciprocal rank fusion. So whether you describe it, upload a photo, or both — we find it."

---

## SCENE 6: VENDOR AI DASHBOARD (1:25 — 1:45)
**Visual:** Vendor dashboard with animated widgets.
- Revenue metric card with sparkline
- Vendor types: "What were my top products last month?"
- Dynamic bar chart generates instantly
- Forecast chart with confidence bands
- Stockout alert: "Order 500 units by June 5th"

**Text on screen:** "AI-Powered Vendor Intelligence"

**VOICEOVER:**
"For vendors, we built an AI dashboard that thinks. Ask any business question in plain English — and get charts, tables, and insights instantly. Our demand forecasting engine predicts sales, detects anomalies, and tells you exactly when to restock — accounting for festivals, weather, and trends."

---

## SCENE 7: THE TECH STACK (1:45 — 1:55)
**Visual:** Technology icons animate in a grid.
- Next.js 14
- FastAPI
- LangGraph
- Qdrant
- BGE-M3 / SigLIP
- Prophet / LightGBM / NeuralForecast
- Stripe

**Text on screen:** "Built for Scale"

**VOICEOVER:**
"Built on Next.js, FastAPI, LangGraph, and Qdrant. Powered by state-of-the-art embeddings and an ensemble forecasting engine. Secure payments through Stripe."

---

## SCENE 8: IMPACT & DIFFERENTIATION (1:55 — 2:10)
**Visual:** Side-by-side comparison.
- Traditional: Search → Filter → Scroll → Filter → Abandon
- Intent Commerce: Talk → Discover → Buy → Done

**Text overlays:**
- "90% faster product discovery"
- "Intelligent inventory decisions"
- "Seamless checkout experience"

**VOICEOVER:**
"Traditional e-commerce is search-and-hope. Intent Commerce is ask-and-receive. For shoppers, discovery is effortless. For vendors, decisions are data-driven. This isn't just a marketplace — it's commerce that understands intent."

---

## SCENE 9: THE VISION (2:10 — 2:30)
**Visual:** Globe with glowing connection points. Users and vendors connected through AI.
**Text on screen:** "INTENT COMMERCE" → "Where Intent Meets Action"

**VOICEOVER:**
"Intent Commerce. Where every conversation leads to the perfect product. Where every vendor knows tomorrow's demand today. This is the future of e-commerce. And it's already here."

**Fade to black. Logo. Tagline.**

## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Visual Style
- Dark background (#0f0f0f) with neon accents (#00d4ff cyan, #a855f7 purple)
- Glassmorphism UI elements
- Smooth 60fps animations
- Modern sans-serif typography (Inter or SF Pro)

### Audio
- Background music: Upbeat electronic, 120 BPM, builds throughout
- Sound effects: Soft chimes for UI interactions, subtle whoosh for transitions
- Voiceover: Clear, confident, slightly energetic

### Pacing
- Scenes 1-2: Fast cuts, problem urgency
- Scene 3: Pause on logo reveal, impact moment
- Scenes 4-6: Smooth, demo-style flow
- Scenes 7-9: Accelerating to climax
